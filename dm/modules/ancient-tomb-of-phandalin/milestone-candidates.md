## Candidate 1: Rescue Rewalt Mason

**Trigger:** The party defeats the skeletons in the Tomb Entrance and persuades/allows Rewalt Mason to exit the Office of Records.

**Rationale:** This is the first significant social resolution of the module and provides the party with their primary intelligence briefing. It marks the end of the surface/Floor 1 opening act and the transition to informed dungeon exploration.

**Source reference:** PDF p. 3, "2. Tomb Office of Records" — Rewalt's rescue and debrief section.

---

## Candidate 2: Descend to Floor 2

**Trigger:** The party defeats the two Skeleton Captains and descends the staircase into the caverns.

**Rationale:** The Skeleton Captains are the Floor 1 boss encounter and gatekeepers to Floor 2. Defeating them marks the transition from the lit, administrative upper tomb to the dark, organic caverns and represents a meaningful escalation point.

**Source reference:** PDF p. 4, "5. Skeleton Captain Chamber."

---

## Candidate 3: Defeat Kodor Drannon (quest completion)

**Trigger:** Kodor Drannon is defeated. All remaining undead in the tomb collapse. The party returns to the surface and reports to the guards.

**Rationale:** This is the module's explicit resolution beat — the quest is complete, all undead are destroyed, and the city guard pays the reward. The source explicitly frames this as the quest completion moment. The party is also expected to advance from 1st to 2nd level by the module's end; this is the natural milestone point for that advancement.

**Source reference:** PDF p. 6, "Afterward" section; PDF p. 1, "A 3-hour adventure for 1st level characters."
