## Hook 1: The Guard Captain's Commission

A captain of the city guard seeks adventurers to investigate a disturbance at an old tomb on the outskirts of town. Two citizens were recently attacked by a zombie in the nearby cemetery; one escaped and alerted two city guards who destroyed the zombie. The city guard is stretched thin and does not want to risk more guard lives. The captain offers 15 GP per character upon completion — defined as stopping whatever is causing the dead to leave the tomb and reporting back to the guards posted at the cemetery entrance.

The captain escorts the party to the cemetery personally and can provide the following context before they descend:
- The tomb has existed since before Phandalin was rebuilt.
- The city guard has told people the tomb is cursed to keep people from entering and hurting themselves.
- They believe the tomb belonged to a mage named Kodor Drannon, but records from before the town's resettlement are sparse.
