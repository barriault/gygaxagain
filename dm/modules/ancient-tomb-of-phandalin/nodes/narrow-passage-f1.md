## Description

A long hall approximately 90 feet long. Short 20-foot hallways branch left and right every 30 feet. The walls from floor to ceiling are filled with alcoves where skeletons rest. The hallway is narrow enough that the party can only move in rows of three.

Accessed through the right door from the Tomb Entrance.

## NPCs present

None (undead only).

## Notable features

- Skeleton ambush trap: Between the first and second branch hallways, skeletons crawl out of the alcoves — two from each end of the hallway — unless a character passes a DC 15 Wisdom (Perception) check before moving into that section and notices two skeletons stirring near the first set of hallways.
- The narrow width forces tactical positioning; the party can only engage in rows of three.

## Encounter

**Hostile Creatures:** 4 Skeletons (MM p. 272), 200 XP total.

Tactics: Two emerge from the near end, two from the far end, catching the party in a pincer if undetected.

## Treasure / outcomes

No treasure in this passage.

## Exits / connections

- West door → Tomb Entrance (tomb-entrance-f1).
- East end → Skeleton Captain Chamber (skeleton-captain-chamber-f1).
