## Description

The tunnels that split at the cavern entrance converge again into a larger tunnel leading to a large set of doors. The room beyond has a hexagon shape. In the center is an ornate sarcophagus decorated with etchings of the god Myrkul, now open. To the left is a now-empty weapons rack. To the right is a large chest. At the back of the room is a stone altar. Kodor Drannon stands near the altar, reading from a book resting on it. He has a skeletal form, wears a black hooded robe, and has a dark blue glow emanating from his eyes. Two skeletons stand in the center of the room near the sarcophagus. The party is noticed upon entering.

## NPCs present

**Kodor Drannon** — Undead mage (custom stat block — see secrets.md). Skeletal form, black hooded robe, dark blue glowing eyes. Was a powerful mage in life; now an undead spellcaster serving Myrkul. He says little before attacking. Tactics: Kodor hangs back and casts spells while the two skeletons engage in melee.

## Notable features

- The stone altar holds a book of Myrkul's dogma. If the party inspects the altar and altar symbol, they can recognize it as the symbol of Myrkul, god of the dead. The book contains Myrkul's instructions to make all fear him in exchange for great power and eternal life.
- The opened ornate sarcophagus was Kodor's; the wave of energy from its opening awakened Kodor and all the dead in the tomb.
- The empty weapons rack was presumably looted before or by Kodor.
- The large chest (right side) contains significant treasure (see below).
- When Kodor is defeated, all undead remaining in the tomb are immediately destroyed and fall to the ground.

## Encounter

**Hostile Creatures:**
- Kodor Drannon (custom stat block — see secrets.md), 200 XP
- 2 Skeletons (MM p. 272), 100 XP total

Tactics: Kodor casts spells from the back (ray of frost, magic missile, hold person, etc.) while skeletons engage the party in melee. Kodor uses shield reactively and misty step to reposition if cornered.

## Treasure / outcomes

Large chest contents: 500 cp, 250 sp, 40 gp, elegant robe (15 gp), blue quartz gemstone (10 gp), pewter crown (25 gp), Carved Bone Statuette of Myrkul (25 gp), Malachite gemstone (10 gp), +1 Shield, Potion of Healing.

**Quest completion:** Defeating Kodor Drannon ends the adventure. All undead in the tomb are destroyed. Returning to the surface and reporting to the guards completes the quest and awards each party member 200 XP and 15 GP from the city.

Additional XP: 15 XP per character for each trap successfully disarmed or avoided.
