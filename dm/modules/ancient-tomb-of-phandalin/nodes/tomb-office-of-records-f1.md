## Description

A locked room off the left door of the Tomb Entrance. Inside is a human thief named Rewalt Mason, who locked himself in to escape the awakening undead. Rewalt will not open the door until told the skeletons outside have been defeated.

## NPCs present

**Rewalt Mason** — Human thief, approximately 40 years old, visibly unkempt (described as appearing not to have bathed in weeks). He is hesitant and nervous even after letting the party in.

Rewalt's knowledge (will share if asked):
- He and his Halfling friend Leodak broke into the tomb after hearing it was the burial place of a powerful mage buried with lots of treasure.
- On the second floor they began opening sarcophagi to remove treasure from the bodies.
- When they opened Kodor Drannon's sarcophagus, a large wave of energy burst from it and knocked them to the floor. They saw Kodor rise from his sarcophagus.
- They fled but the dead around them were starting to awaken. Rewalt was cut off by skeletons at the exit and had to lock himself in this room.
- He does not know what happened to Leodak after they were separated on the second floor.
- Rewalt's party encountered traps on the second floor; he believes most can be avoided if you don't try to steal anything.

Rewalt's promise: after being rescued he agrees to turn himself in to the guards at the tomb entrance. (He does not follow through — see secrets.md.)

## Notable features

- The door is locked from the inside. Rewalt controls access.
- The room is labeled "Office of Records" — its original administrative purpose for the tomb.

## Encounter

No hostile creatures.

## Treasure / outcomes

Rewalt provides critical intelligence about Kodor Drannon's awakening and the second floor's dangers.
