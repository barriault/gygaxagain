## Description

Read-aloud text: "Walking down the staircase, you find yourself in a large circular room. The room is well lit, with torches lined up on the stone walls. Three wooden doors can be seen to your left, right, and in front of you. Two skeletons are banging on the left door, trying to get to whatever is on the other side."

The room is circular, well-lit by wall torches, with three wooden doors: left, right, and forward (north).

## NPCs present

None (undead only).

## Notable features

- Two skeletons are actively attacking the left door (the door to the Tomb Office of Records, where Rewalt Mason is locked inside).
- The skeletons will not notice the characters at first but will react if any character fails a Stealth check while approaching or stands in place for too long.
- Left door: leads to Tomb Office of Records (node: tomb-office-of-records-f1).
- Right door: leads to Narrow Passage (node: narrow-passage-f1).
- Forward (north) door: leads to Crematorium (node: crematorium-f1).

## Encounter

**Hostile Creatures:** 2 Skeletons (MM p. 272), 100 XP total.

Tactics: The skeletons are focused on the left door when the party enters. They turn hostile if the party fails Stealth or lingers.

## Treasure / outcomes

No treasure in this room.

## Exits / connections

- Left door → Tomb Office of Records (tomb-office-of-records-f1)
- Right door → Narrow Passage (narrow-passage-f1)
- North door → Crematorium (crematorium-f1)
- Staircase up → Cemetery Exterior (cemetery-exterior)
