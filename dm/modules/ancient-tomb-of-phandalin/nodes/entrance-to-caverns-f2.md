## Description

Read-aloud text: "The staircase leads to a cavern that splits into narrow tunnels going to the left and right. A male Halfling body is lying at the bottom of the stairs with multiple claw and bite marks on his body. Ahead you can see the walls of each tunnel are filled with alcoves where the dead are resting."

Floor 2 is entirely unlit. Characters require torches or darkvision to see. This is the entry point and junction for the two cavern tunnels.

## NPCs present

None living. The Halfling body is Leodak — Rewalt's companion (see secrets.md).

## Notable features

- No traps or hostile creatures at this node.
- The Halfling body can be searched: yields a dagger, 3 gp, and a silver necklace worth 5 sp.
- The cavern splits into two hallways at the far end: Left Tunnel and Right Tunnel.
- Walls are lined with burial alcoves throughout.

## Encounter

None.

## Treasure / outcomes

From Leodak's body: dagger, 3 gp, silver necklace (5 sp).

## Exits / connections

- Staircase up → Skeleton Captain Chamber, Floor 1 (skeleton-captain-chamber-f1).
- Left branch → Left Tunnel (left-tunnel-f2).
- Right branch → Right Tunnel (right-tunnel-f2).
