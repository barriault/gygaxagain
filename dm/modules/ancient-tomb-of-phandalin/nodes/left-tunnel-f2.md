## Description

The left branch from the cavern entrance splits again into two tunnels (left and right). The left sub-tunnel leads 20 feet to an open sarcophagus. To the left of the sarcophagus is a skeleton that has already been killed — it has sprung a trap and its skull has been impaled by a spear protruding from the wall. In front of the sarcophagus sits a wooden chest. The right sub-tunnel leads to a long tunnel connecting to the cavern exit (toward Kodor's Resting Place).

## NPCs present

None.

## Notable features

- The impaled skeleton serves as a visible warning that a spear trap existed and has been triggered.
- Searching the skeleton: a silver ring worth 5 sp.
- The wooden chest has a crossbow trap attached: fires an arrow dealing 1d10 damage.
  - Detection: DC 15 Wisdom (Perception) to notice the trap.
  - Disarm: DC 10 Dexterity check.
- The wooden chest contains 100 sp.
- The right sub-tunnel is a long tunnel leading to the cavern exit (connects toward Kodor's Resting Place — see connections.md for routing).

## Encounter

No hostile creatures in this node.

## Treasure / outcomes

- Silver ring (5 sp) from the impaled skeleton.
- Wooden chest: 100 sp (if trap disarmed or triggered and survived).
