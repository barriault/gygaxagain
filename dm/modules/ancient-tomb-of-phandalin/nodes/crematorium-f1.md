## Description

Read-aloud text: "You find yourself in a large crematorium. A pungent odor fills the room from the corpses that have been in this room through the years and every surface in the room is covered in a layer of dust. On your left are four wooden tables for bodies to lie on before they are put in the furnace that you see in front of you, at the back of the room. On your right looks to be a table with a pile of items removed from bodies before they were cremated."

Accessed through the north door from the Tomb Entrance.

## NPCs present

None (undead only).

## Notable features

- Four wooden body tables on the left.
- A large furnace at the back of the room.
- A table on the right holding items removed from bodies prior to cremation.
- The furnace can be lit by igniting the wood pile underneath the slab where bodies lie. Any zombie pushed into the lit furnace is destroyed instantly.

## Encounter

**Hostile Creatures:** 3 Zombies (MM p. 316), 150 XP total.

Tactics: Zombies wander the room. They were awakened before their bodies could be cremated. Environmental interaction: the furnace can be used to destroy zombies that are pushed into it while lit.

## Treasure / outcomes

Searching the items table: 20 arrows, two short swords, and a copper ring worth 15 cp.

## Exits / connections

- South door → Tomb Entrance (tomb-entrance-f1).
