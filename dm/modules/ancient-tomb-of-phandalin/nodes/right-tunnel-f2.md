## Description

The right branch from the cavern entrance leads to a 60-foot tunnel that the party must traverse in a line. Halfway through, a magic missile trap fires from a skull statue at the end of the tunnel. The tunnel opens into a round room containing an open sarcophagus on the left and another tunnel on the right leading to the cavern exit. In the center of the round room is a Ghoul.

## NPCs present

None living.

## Notable features

- Magic missile trap: located halfway through the 60-foot tunnel, fired by a skull statue at the tunnel's end.
  - Detection: DC 10 Wisdom (Perception) to find.
  - Disarm: DC 10 Dexterity to disable.
  - If not disabled: the person in front takes 1d10 force damage.
- The open sarcophagus in the round room is already open (previously looted).
- The right tunnel from the round room leads to the cavern exit toward Kodor's Resting Place.
- The Ghoul is in the center of the round room.

## Encounter

**Hostile Creature:** 1 Ghoul (MM p. 148), 200 XP.

Tactics: The Ghoul is in the round room; it engages the party when they enter.

## Treasure / outcomes

- Searching the Ghoul after defeating it: a gold ring worth 5 gp.

## Exits / connections

- Back (west/north) → Entrance to Caverns junction (entrance-to-caverns-f2).
- Right tunnel from the round room → cavern exit leading toward Kodor's Resting Place (kodors-resting-place-f2) — see connections.md.
