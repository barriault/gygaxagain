## Description

A circular room mirroring the Tomb Entrance at the far end of the Narrow Passage. To the left and front are doors leading to small rooms containing opened sarcophagi (already looted). To the right is a staircase leading down to Floor 2. In the center of the room stand two Skeleton Captains.

## NPCs present

None (undead only).

## Notable features

- Doors to the left and ahead lead to small side rooms with opened, empty sarcophagi (already plundered by Rewalt and Leodak).
- The staircase to the right descends to Floor 2: Entrance to Caverns.

## Encounter

**Hostile Creatures:** 2 Skeleton Captains (custom stat block — see secrets.md), 200 XP total.

Tactics: The Skeleton Captains use Multiattack and engage in melee. They guard the staircase to the lower floor.

## Treasure / outcomes

No treasure in the main chamber (sarcophagus side rooms are already looted).

## Exits / connections

- West → Narrow Passage (narrow-passage-f1).
- Left/forward doors → small sarcophagus side rooms (no encounters, already looted, no further exits).
- Staircase right/down → Floor 2: Entrance to Caverns (entrance-to-caverns-f2).
