## Description

An old cemetery on the outskirts of Phandalin, appearing to be a couple of centuries old. Headstones are weathered and unreadable; vines cover the sides of a tomb at the back of the cemetery. Two soldiers guard the tomb entrance at the staircase leading down.

## NPCs present

**City Guard Captain** — the quest-giver. Escorts the party here. Can provide background on the tomb (see hooks.md). Departs before the party descends; guards remain posted at the entrance.

**Two City Guard Soldiers** — stationed at the tomb entrance. They remain here throughout the adventure. Rewalt Mason will surrender to these guards after rescue (though he later reneges — see secrets.md).

## Notable features

- The tomb entrance is a staircase leading down into the earth, partially obscured by vines.
- The cemetery has not been actively used or maintained in memory.
