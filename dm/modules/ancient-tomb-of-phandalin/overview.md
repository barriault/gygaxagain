## Summary

The adventurers are hired by a city guard captain to investigate why undead are attacking villagers near a cemetery on the outskirts of Phandalin. The cemetery contains an ancient tomb that predates the town's resettlement. Inside the two-floor tomb, the party discovers that a powerful undead mage named Kodor Drannon has been awakened from his sarcophagus — disturbed by two thieves who broke in seeking buried treasure. Kodor is animating the dead throughout the tomb and driving undead to attack the town. Defeating Kodor causes all remaining undead to collapse instantly. The quest resolves when the party returns to the surface and reports to the guard captain.

The module is designed for 1st level play with a party of four or five characters and runs approximately 2-4 hours. It can be placed near any human settlement in place of Phandalin.

## Recommended hooks

See hooks.md. The primary hook is a paid commission from the city guard captain (15 GP per character on completion).

## Setting & tone

A two-floor dungeon crawl set in a centuries-old tomb. Floor 1 uses torchlit stone corridors with a clinical, abandoned administrative feel (office of records, crematorium). Floor 2 drops to unlit natural caverns requiring torches or darkvision — a shift toward claustrophobic dread. The final confrontation is in a hexagonal burial chamber decorated with etchings of Myrkul, god of the dead. Tone is horror-tinged dungeon adventure appropriate for new players.
