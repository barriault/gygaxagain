# Cult of Myrkul (Kodor Drannon's Thrall)

## Identity

Kodor Drannon was a powerful wizard in life who pledged himself to Myrkul, god of the dead, in exchange for eternal life and great power. He received both, in the form of undeath. His "cult" is not an organized brotherhood of living worshippers but rather the necrotic order he enforces from his tomb: the animated dead of the tomb are his congregation, animated and held under his will, and the altar of Myrkul in his burial chamber is his temple. Whether Kodor's pact with Myrkul extends to living agents elsewhere in the world is not established by the module — what is established is that the doctrine (spread fear of Myrkul; receive power and eternal life) is active and inscribed.

## Active operation

Kodor Drannon, awakened from his sarcophagus by thieves disturbing it, is animating every corpse in the ancient tomb beneath Phandalin's cemetery and driving waves of undead to attack the surrounding town. His purpose is not territorial — it is doctrinal: spreading fear of Myrkul, as the altar-book instructs. Every villager attacked is a message.
