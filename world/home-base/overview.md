# Home base: Amphail

A small village a day north of Waterdeep, set among rolling farmland and
dotted with ancient stone fences. Amphail's reputation is for terse, hard-
working farmers who don't trust outsiders quickly and whose nights are
unusually quiet — quieter than a village this size should be. People here
have clearly fallen on hard times.

The village's center is **The Gilded Stallion**, a low-beamed roadside
tavern with a slate roof and a faded carving of a horse over the door.
Travellers stop here for ale and a bed; locals come for the gossip.

## Notable NPCs

- **Ravenna** — barmaid at The Gilded Stallion. Reputed jolly, talkative,
  knows the regulars by name. Newer to town than most.
