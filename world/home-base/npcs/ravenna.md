# Ravenna

The barmaid at The Gilded Stallion. Newer to Amphail than the regulars —
arrived in the village within the past few months, took up the job at the
tavern after the old keeper's daughter married into a farm family south
of town.

## Description

A wiry woman in her late twenties, dark hair pulled back, sleeves rolled
to the elbow. Quick laugh, quick step, easy with the regulars. Wears a
plain apron over a homespun dress. Her hands are calloused — she handles
casks all day, after all.

## Public-known facts

- Has worked at The Gilded Stallion for a few months.
- Known to be from "down the coast somewhere" — most locals haven't asked
  for specifics, and she hasn't volunteered.
- Greets every traveler with "what'll it be, friend?" and remembers your
  drink the second time.
- Generally cheerful. Picks up on the regulars' moods.

## Mannerisms

- Wipes the bar with the same rag for hours; calls it "Old Reliable."
- Asks travelers where they're headed before she asks where they're from.
- Laughs easily, but the laugh doesn't always reach her eyes.
